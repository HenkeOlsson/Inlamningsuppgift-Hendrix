"Alla vill till himmelen men ingen vill dö" is Timbuktu's fourth studio album. The album was released on February 23, 2005, on the record label JuJu Records. The record sold gold in Sweden and Norway and it features many other famous artists. The first singel from the record was "Alla vill till himmelen men ingen vill dö" and it was recorded in Ghana.

The record was ranked by Sonic Magazine in June 2013 as the 26th best album ever in Sweden.

The song "Alla vill till himmelen men ingen vill dö" is taken from the American artist Loretta Lynn's "Everybody wants to go to heaven but nobody wants to die", which was released in 1965 and is to a large amount a translation of the original.

The second and the third single were "Det löser sig", together with Chords and Supreme, which became a big hit in Sweden, and "Stirra ner".
