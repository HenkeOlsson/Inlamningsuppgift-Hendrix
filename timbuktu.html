"Alla vill till himmelen men ingen vill dö" is Timbuktu's fourth studio album. The album was released on February 23, 2005, on the record label JuJu Records. The record sold gold in Sweden and Norway and it features many other famous artists. The record was ranked by Sonic Magazine in June 2013 as the 26th best album ever in Sweden.
